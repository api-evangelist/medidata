Reading Time: 11 minutes Clean, consistent, high-quality data is the lifeblood of a clinical trial. Clinical data management (CDM) is the collection, validation, quality management, integration, and delivery of clinical data to deliver high-quality, reliable data for statistical analysis, regulatory compliance and submission, medical decision-making, and further clinical research. Originally, CDM was manual, paper-based, and error-prone.
