AI in clinical trials is now an operational reality rather than a future bet. Drawing on the 2nd Annual State of AI in Clinical Trials survey conducted with Everest Group among senior clinical operations leaders across pharma, biotech, and CROs, the post reports that roughly one-third of organizations are actively deploying AI across a handful or the majority of their trial activities.
